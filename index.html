MOLARS BLOOM BEHIND THE PARTITION

On a clear new years' day, 10,000 dessicated corpses spawned above New Seattle and smashed against the ground like rain. Most of them resembled humans. They were evenly spaced at first. When they landed, bouncing off cars and rolling down highways, there was no longer a pattern. People sought to connect the dots regardless - like constellations.

The bodies heralded the arrival of New Rules.
